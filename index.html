welcome Note

St Michaels School was founded in 2003 and is one of the best Schools in Kenya. Our kindergarten has established a reputation for excellence in academic and continues to forge ahead as a leader in early years. Because of our solid foundation we felt we have opened doors to Junior School in Kitui county and for all Kenyans. Currently we have grade one to class 8 classes.We are
