Head teacher Comments

Our nursing department provides a good
health monitor for children. Young
children encounter various health
challenges as their immunity develops.
This calls for frequent monitoring and this
is where the residential nurse comes
in handy. Parents receive real time
update on the health progress of their
learners from this desk.
